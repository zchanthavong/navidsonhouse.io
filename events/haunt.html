Haunted House Event

Every Halloween between 5 and 9 PM we like to turn this place in to a haunted house. If you're ever trick-or-treating in the area, feel free to come in and experience the haunt. Wander through while getting chased by spooky scary skeletons, brain hangry zombies, the minotaur, bed sheet ghosts, and many more.

The bed and breakfast is closed during the event.
